About Me

Turning complex data into meaningful business decisions.

I'm Edivaldo Andrade, a Data Analyst with a strong passion for transforming data into meaningful insights that drive smarter business decisions.

My academic background includes a Bachelor's degree in Business Management and a Master's degree in Business Analytics, providing me with a solid foundation in business, analytics and technology.

I currently work as a Fraud Data Analyst where I analyse fraud-related data, develop reporting solutions, automate processes and build dashboards that support fraud prevention and operational efficiency.

Beyond analytics, I build AI-powered applications and workflow automations that help businesses improve productivity and create measurable value.

My technical toolkit includes SQL, Power BI, Excel, Python, Power Apps, n8n and Make while continuously expanding my expertise in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Automation.

Outside of my professional role, I founded The Data Explorer, a growing educational platform where I share tutorials, practical projects, career advice and learning resources for aspiring data and AI professionals.

Through my content, I enjoy simplifying technical concepts and helping others develop the skills needed to succeed in data analytics, machine learning and AI automation.
